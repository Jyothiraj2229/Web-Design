The Registrar is the administrative head of the University. she is appointed by the Executive Council. She is the custodian of the University properties and is the person to sue and to be sued. She is in overall charge of the administration and its finances, and oversees the implementation policies and programmes in the University. Her powers and functions are clearly defined in the statutes of the University. She acts as the Secretary of the Executive Council and Academic Senate.
