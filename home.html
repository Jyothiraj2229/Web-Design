Welcome to RajanSri University:

RajanSri University is named after its founder,karthiksrirajan.who rather through a royalcharter,brought the university into existence in 1885.The establishment of rajanuniversity symbolizes a renaissance in the indian educational system.It has significantly contributed to the academic and economic development of not only the region but also of the Country. It has a vast sprawling campus set in a picturesque and idyllic surroundings. Buildings of great architectural elegance and variety enhance its beauty.
